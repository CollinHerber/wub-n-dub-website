Re-Entry

Car camping Re-entry will be allowed between 10am and 2pm. Tent campers will be allowed to leave and come back throughout weekend. Offsite parking after 1st entry.

Acceptable and Unacceptable forms of identification

Acceptable forms are any U.S. government issued driver’s license or identification card provided that it contains a photograph, name, and date of birth. A U.S. or foreign government issued passport containing a photograph and date of birth. Unacceptable forms of identification include; school identification, work identification, birth certificate, unofficial copies of any identification, or a social security card.

Food/Ice/water

Ice will be available for purchase on site. Water will be free and readily available at multiple locations on site. There will be a variety of food vendors on site throughout the weekend.

Fire Policy:

Campfires are strictly prohibited. There will be a large supervised fire within the venue grounds. Fire spinning is allowed within a 50’ radius of the fire pit only. (No rogue fire within the campgrounds!)

Service Dog Policy

Service dogs are permitted, emotional support animals are not. A service animal performs specific, trained tasks to mitigate a specific disability. Service dogs must be marked and leashed at all times. Service dogs must always be in “full stay” when not performing the service for which they have been trained. Service animals in training are not service animals and therefore not permitted to attend the festival.

Zero Tolerance Drug Policy

Drugs and drug paraphernalia are strictly prohibited.

Medical

A centrally located medical tent will be staffed with an emergency medical team. Medical personnel will also be roaming the grounds throughout the weekend. Look for security, official staff, or harm reduction personnel to assist you in an emergency.

Security

Every car entering the grounds will be thoroughly searched in an effort to locate and remove prohibited items. Attendees are also subject to search. Security officers reserve the right to deny entry to anyone.

Leave No Trace

This land should be left in the same if not better condition than we found it! Trash bags will be provided to you upon entering the festival and should be used for your campsite. Trash cans will be located throughout the festival. DO NOT throw cigarette butts on the ground. Trash bags will be collected from campsites at the end of the weekend.
